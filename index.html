About us

Pedal to the Metal was started after the owner, discovered that there was a gap in the market in the West Lothian areas of Winchburgh and Broxburn, located on the outskirts of Edinburgh.
The business was founded in 2019 and became a storefront in 2020 and the business has been on a growing bicycle repair and stockist since then.

We specialise in building beautiful custom bespoke mountain bikes from hardtails to enduro bikes that will always turn heads wherever you go.

If you don't want a bespoke custom built bike, not to worry! Pedal to the Metal deal with top industry bike brands such as, NS Bikes, Ghost Bikes, FUJI and Blank. Along with bike parts from, Title, SRAM, Rockshox and Profile.

Pedal to the Metal are here to provide bikes for everyone, from kids getting their first bike, beginners just starting out to individuals who have been riding for years and are looking for top of the range products and service.

Here at Pedal to the Metal we strive to keep everyone rolling!

Meet the staff

The staff here at Pedal to the Metal bikes have over 25 years of mountain bike riding experience from sponsorship riding to riding for the sheer enjoyment of it! From racking up the miles on a road bike, riding a hardtail bike catching big air in the skatepark to trail/dh bikes shredding up the forest and sending big jumps, the staff have a well rounded knowledge of all types of bikes, and how to fix them!

Whether it's bringing your bike in for repair or just dropping by to talk all things bike, the guys at Pedal to the Metal are keen to meet you!

Murray

Owner / Mechanic

Murray has worked on all kinds of bicycle over the years, and is a very passionate jump bike and mtb rider. He is the shop manager and owner of PTTM.
Murray is an avid guitar player, you never know, you may get to hear him shred in the shop if you drop by on his lunch break. He rides skateparks more for the majority of the time and loves to build bespoke mountain bikes as a side hobby.
Murray has previously been sponsored by various brands including Kona UK.
